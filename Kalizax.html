Kalizax the Vile

Large Dragon (Chromatic), Chaotic Evil

  • Armor Class 18
  • Hit Points 127 (15d10 + 45)
  • Speed 40 ft., Fly 80 ft., Swim 40 ft.
  • Initiative +5 (15)
STR DEX CON INT WIS CHA
19 (+4) 14 (+2) 17 (+3) 12 (+1) 11 (+0) 15 (+2)
  • Saving Throws Dex +5, Wis +3
  • Skills Perception +6, Stealth +8
  • Immunities Acid
  • Senses Blindsight 30 ft., Darkvision 120 ft., Passive Perception 16
  • Languages Common, Draconic
  • Challenge 7 (XP 2,900; PB +3)

Traits

Amphibious. Kalizax can breathe air and water.

Stalker. Kalizax doubles his proficiency bonus made to Dexterity (Stealth) checks. Additionally, he can attempt to hide while only lightly obscured.

Actions

Multiattack. Kalizax makes three Rend attacks, he can replace one attack with either a grapple attempt (reach: 10ft., DC: 15) or a use of spellcasting to cast Sorcerous Burst (acid only).

Rend. Melee Attack Roll: +7, reach 10 ft. Hit: 9 (2d4+4) Slashing damage plus 3 (1d6) Acid damage.

Acid Breath (Recharge 5–6). Dexterity Saving Throw: DC 14, each creature in a 30-foot-long, 5-foot-wide Line. Failure: 49 (14d6) Acid damage. Success: Half damage.

Spellcasting. Kalizax casts one of the following spells, requiring no Material components and using Charisma as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 13, +5 to hit with spell attacks):

At will: Sorcerous Burst (acid only)
1/day each: Invisibility

Bonus Actions

Misty Step (1/day). Kalizax casts Misty Step, using the same spellcasting ability as Spellcasting.

Hide. Kalizax takes the Hide action.

Villain Actions

Kalizax has three villain actions. He can take each action once during an encounter after an enemy’s turn. He can take these actions in any order but can use only one per round.

Action 1: Ascend. Kalizax moves up to half his flying speed straight up.
Action 2: Pounce. Kalizax can move up to half its speed, and it makes one Rend attack.
Action 3: Acid Mist. Kalizax exudes a stationary acidic smog in a 30 ft. emanation. The smog hangs in the air for 1 minute. The area of the smog is lightly obscured, and creatures besides Kalizax who start their turn inside the smog or enter it for the first time in a round make a DC 14 Constitution saving throw or spend their bonus action on their turn coughing and retching. A creature who succeeds on such a save is immune the smog for the rest of its duration.
